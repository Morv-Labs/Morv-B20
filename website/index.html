CLI

Full B20 surface

Read, deploy, mint, policies, and roles — with correct params encoding and initCalls.

b20 status

Beryl activation + factory health check

b20 deploy

Asset/Stablecoin wizard with supply cap, minter, policies

b20 inspect

Auto-detect variant, policy labels, role matrix

b20 mint

Mint with MINT_ROLE

b20 policy attach

Bind TRANSFER_SENDER_POLICY etc.

b20 role grant

grantRole for any B20 role

b20 address-decode

Offline variant byte decode

b20 policy create

Blocklist / allowlist on PolicyRegistry
